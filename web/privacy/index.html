Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

Tathmfy builds on-device credit identity for Africa. This page covers tathmfy.com only — not the mobile app, which keeps your financial data on your phone.

What we collect

On this website, we collect:

  • Email address — if you join the waitlist
  • Basic technical data — browser type, approximate region, pages visited (standard server logs from our host)

We do not collect payment details, credit scores, or transaction history through this site.

Why we collect it

Your email is used to tell you about early access and product updates. Server logs help us keep the site secure and working.

Where it is stored

Waitlist emails are processed through our form provider and delivered to us by email. They are not sold or rented to third parties.

How long we keep it

Waitlist emails are kept until you ask us to remove them, or until they are no longer needed for launch communications.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from waitlist emails at any time. To request access or deletion of your email, contact us below.
